### Hi-Campus – Feature List

1. **Authentication (Login/Logout)**

- **Email OTP login**: Students can log in using a one-time password sent to their email.
- **Kakao login**: Integration with Kakao OAuth for local students.
- **Logout**: Clear session/token securely.

2. **Community**

- **Post & Comment**: Students can create posts and add comments.
- **Moderation**: AI filters inappropriate language or spam.
- **Summary (AI)**: Long posts can be summarized automatically.
- **Search**: Natural language search for posts and comments.

3. **Campus Info**

- **Information database**: Cafeterias, study rooms, lounges, restrooms, etc.
- **Multi-language translation (AI)**: Information is automatically translated into Korean, English, and Vietnamese.
- **Campus Q&A Chatbot**: Students can ask questions (e.g., “How to register for a class?”) and get instant answers.

4. **Jobs & Career**

- **Job recommendations (AI)**: Suggest part-time jobs, internships, or events based on user profile and skills.
- **Explanation**: Each recommendation comes with a short reason ("This matches your Flutter skills and location in Daegu").
- **CV assistant (AI, Phase 2)**: Suggest improvements in resumes.
- **Interview prep bot (AI, Phase 2)**: Mock interview questions for practice.

5. **Matching & Language Exchange**

- **Helper matching**: Match international students with Korean “student helpers.”
- **Language exchange**: Pair students based on language preferences.
- **Slang dictionary (AI)**: Translate Korean slang and provide examples.
- **Smart matching (AI, Phase 2)**: Use AI to suggest best partners based on schedule and interests.

6. **General Features**

- **Multi-language UI**: English, Korean, and Vietnamese supported.
- **Cross-platform**: Single Flutter codebase → iOS, Android, Web, and macOS.
- **Push notifications**: For new posts, comments, or job updates.
- **Personalized timeline (AI, Phase 2)**: Reminder for deadlines, events, and job opportunities.
